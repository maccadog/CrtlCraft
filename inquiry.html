Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our customization process

How long does the process take?

Standard timeline is 3-4 weeks from approval to delivery. Rush orders (1-2 weeks) are available for an additional fee.

Do you provide a warranty?

Yes! All our finishes come with a lifetime warranty against defects in materials and workmanship.

Can you match specific colors?

Absolutely! We can match any color using professional color matching systems and custom paint mixing.

Do you work with all controller types?

We specialize in Xbox, PlayStation, and Nintendo controllers, but can work with most gaming controllers. Contact us for custom requests.

What if I'm not satisfied with the result?

Customer satisfaction is our priority. We provide progress photos and work with you throughout the process to ensure the final result exceeds expectations.
